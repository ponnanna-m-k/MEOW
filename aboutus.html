Our Vision

Our vision is custom design at scale. Users design their own eyewear from discovering size and shape to choosing the color and material of the frame and lens they order, all from the comfort of their homes.

Personalized Fashion is at the heart of Meow. We transform complex 3D scanning data into size and shape discovery with our patented technology. Each customer brings unique insights; a data-driven sense to inclusive design guides our aesthetics and intuition at work. Our appetite for intelligent design facilitates a culture of experimentation with machine learning and design automation, shaping the future of Fashion and lifestyle design.
